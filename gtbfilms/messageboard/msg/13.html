Film Idea- Holiday Programme

Submitted by Baldas on 20/Apr/2007
127.0.0.1

Message:

A different idea this time. We do a fake holiday programme, presented like all these travel shows where they tell you places to stay and things to do, but we do it about somewhere really awful and try to make out that it is really good. Probably not a real named town (although we COULD go to Cumbernauld and just film it) but I think it would be better if we invented a place name such as Bridge Of Merde or Merdehampton (think French) and used various footage of different places and pretended it was Merdehampton. Or if we want to show the town sign we could make it Skinflats (a real place near Grangemouth which sounds awful, and is).

All done totally straight, as if it was good, but presenting really c*** things to see like taking them on a drive around town and all you see is loads of roundabouts (easily filmed in Livingston, and should be played back at ultra high speed as we whizz around them) or taking them to "the beach" but actually filming that at Grangemouth mudflats (with loads of cooling towers and industry in the background as our presenter trudges through the mud). And for places to stay, I'm sure we can find some really dreadful looking places in or around Edinburgh, then pretend we are inside it and film a room in Magnox's upstairs flat with a camp bed in it. But ALWa**aying how great it is.

It wouldn't need many actors, but it would need careful selection of the various locations and a bit of travelling around to film them. And it might be hard to play it totally straight and not laugh.
