www.GtBFilms.co.uk

Established in 1990, GtBFilms have produced a wide range of productions, from the earliest 'Christmas 1990', to modern-day award-winning classics such as 'The Wish'.

This site is a comprehensive repository of information, behind-the-scenes photos and special features on the people, the ideas, and the development process behind our films, as well as providing visitors with a glimpse of completed productions, and works-in-progress.


Contact GtBFilms - we always pleased to hear from fellow film makers and our viewers!
