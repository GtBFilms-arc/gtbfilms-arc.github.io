The Duel

GtB January 13, 2026

"What a brilliant little film!"
Judging Panel, Edinburgh Cine and Video Society		 Duration: 4 mins 00 secs
Filming: January and February 2011 in 4:3 format
 
The Duel is a short story of musical rivalry between classical musician Rufus Clayman, and his sometimes friend Clyde Platztiseen.

At first the characters are in competition with each other, but they both soon realise that they can have much more fun co-operating with each other rather than competing.

This film was animated using Stop Motion Pro 4, and a Sony Digital 8 camcorder. The characters were made from Plasticene, and scale props were obtained from Ebay.

Interesting Facts:
Originally conceived many years ago, the original intention was to animate this piece of music using computer generated characters, probably animated ants. One would play a classical string instrument, and the other a modern electronic keyboard.
