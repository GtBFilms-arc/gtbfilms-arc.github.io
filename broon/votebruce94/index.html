Vote Bruce 1994!

 
The Bruce for President campaign get underway, with the traditional death defying hanging of Pollock bedsheets from public buildings.
Bruiser attempts to convince a floating voter using a tin opener.
Mad Jen is impressed with the Election propaganda.
For the final hustings, entrance is everything.
The old 'Bruiser drinks Windolene and Gonad eats Ajax' trick never fails to impress.
As does the 'Bruiser pulls rubber chicken out of his trousers' gag!
But for the finale, the 'lighting dynamite in your trousers' gag will never be beaten! And the scars have almost all healed.
