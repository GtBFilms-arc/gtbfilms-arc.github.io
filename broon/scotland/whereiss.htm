Where is Scotland?

                             

This is a good question, and people who have never visited Scotland often get it wrong.

Despite popular opinion Scotland is in fact part of the United Kingdom, not the one stuck on the side of England, that's Wales. If you want to know about Wales, perhaps you're interested in coal mines and singing, then please stop reading now and get hold of the Wales.general FAQ.

                         

                             
Scotland is in fact connected to the top of England. It is not part of England but is in fact a completely seperate country! Luckily most Scottish people now speak ENGLISH, and some of them have even become so anglisised that they have lost their red hair, and wear trousers rather than the more traditional, and still tremendously popular kilt.
