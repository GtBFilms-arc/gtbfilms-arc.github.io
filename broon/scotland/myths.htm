Scottish Myths

                             

Scotland is a very ancient country, and as such has many myths and legends associated with it. These range from the classic 'Nessie' legend to the slightly less believable 'Sean Connery' myth depicted in the popular 'HIGHLANDER' films, which suggested that the only way to kill a Scotsman was to cut his head off.
