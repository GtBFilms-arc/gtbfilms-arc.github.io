Established in 1983, Brown Software has a large back-catalogue of games and applications. No records have been kept regarding numbers of sales, but to this day, Brown Software titles sometimes still appear on pirated shovelware compilation CDs, a mark of true success.

Brown Software primarily produced software for the ZX Spectrum Home computer. Now, thanks to the wonders of technology, you can travel back in time and play some of those classic games which many hoped had been lost for good!
