Mingling and the Murder!

Father Shannon reaches for his beer,
while the other guests start to mingle...
...and exchange tidbits of imporant information...
...catching up on old family news....
...and gossip.
Pete Jackson (far left), a major league ball player
before his retirement, exhanges old stories with
publican Seamus Flannery (back to camera)
Madeline Flannery, accompanied by her demanding
mother Sarah, hands out the drinks.
Seamus Flannery (far left) ponders
his gambling debts as guests mingle.
Maureen Flannery confides in
Father Shannon (centre back)
Wild-child Benjamin Claremont (earings)
discusses matters with Seamus.
A heated argument erupts between Pat and Brennan (far right)
Vicky Flannery, wife of publican Seamus,
(back to camera) discusses matters with Benjamin
and Maureen...
... and then with her mother-in-law, Sarah.
Mingling continues....
...and the guests come and go...
Benjamin consoles himself
with a large glass of wine...
...and tries to avoid catching the eye of Father Shannon....
...but to no avail, Father Shannon dispenses
some valuable moral advice anyway.
Baseball player Pete, suggests it's time for the holiday cake.....
...and Pat agrees wholeheartedly...
....as do Vicky and Maureen.
And Seamus is so pleased, he temporarily
forgets his money problems!
Madeline fetches the cake...
... and Pat goes to fetch his elderly aunt....
...only to find her DEAD in her bedroom!
Pat reveals the sad and traumatic news
of Sarah's death to the other guests....
...while staying admirably calm under the circumstances.
