Sleuthing and Revelation

The shocked guests rush through to
the bedroom....
...to inspect the corpse.
Seamus, like the other family members, is stunned
that his mother has been MURDERED
- and calls the Police!
The Inspector soon arrives...
...armed only with those age-old sleuthing tools,
a magnifying glass and a slice of cake.
Now that the body has been removed,
the guests inspect the crime scene....
...for clues that might incriminate the murderer..
Vicky discovers the pillow is smeared with make-up.....
...Sarah was apparently smothered to death!
Pat insists she was dead when he found her...
...while Brennan struggles to explain why his
police revolver was found at the crime scene.
Seamus is not convinced by their claims...
...while Madeline is shocked at the death of her mother.
Vicky leads the guests back into the living room....
Stoney faced Pat claims he had no motive for
killing his Aunt despite having written her will.
The assorted guests discuss the clues...
Suspicion turns to spaced-out Benjamin!
Police Officer Brennan, is keen to implicate Benjamin....
...while Madeline points out Benjamin had little motive...
....the Inspector and Maureen listen on.
...
...
... The finger of suspicion turns temporarily
to Father Shannon!
...The professional sleuth misses nothing of what is said...
...and yet all suspicions are hidden beneath a
stony expression which reveals nothing.
...
Suspicion turns to Pete...
...who denies withholding information about
his relationship with the victim!
Despite the gravity of the situation,
Benjamin seems unusually cheerful.
Maureen fends off questions from suspicious guests.
Pete makes allegations regarding
Seamus' gambling debts...
...visibly shaken, Seamus denies everything.
Discussions continue....
...
Vicky indignantly refutes claims of her shady past...
...while Madeline choses to avoid difficult questions.
The Inspector is ready to reveal the murderer!
...drum roll please...
The lights go out!
...and the guilty party is revealed...
..to a shocked and expectant silence!
The Inspector reveals the full story....
...Seamus, like the rest of the family,
is an emotional wreck.
