Mage's Spellbook!

Compiled below is a list of all the spells, codes and power requirements in the game of Quest. The spells have been arranged into Skill sections - 10+ SKILL lists spells that can be cast by a mage with a skill greater than 10. This does not mean that a skill 10 mage can always discover the spell however. Generally if a mage's skill lies within the band specified, the mage will have a 25% change of discovering it. This chance increases by 25% by each higher band. I.e. A mage of skill 34 will have a 25% chance of discovering a 30+ SKILL spell, a 50% chance of discovering a 20+ SKILL spell, a 75% chance of discovering a 10+ SKILL spell and a 100% chance of discovering a 0+ SKILL spell. Bear this in mind if your mage does not discover a spell straight away...
