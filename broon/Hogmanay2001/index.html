Stirling Hogmanay 2001/2002 !

 It's that time of year once again,

and this time we're off on an excursion

to Stirling Castle...

...as usual the Festive Spirit flows freely.

Baldas laughs at some baldy guy behind him,

knowing his secret is safe....

There are many distractions...

...while we wait for the fireworks.

Ila practices her medical grip technique...

..only with great practice has she

mastered the 'right hand behind the back'

 surprise variation.

The fireworks start on

the stroke of midnight!

Happy New Year!

Choccy swigs neat vodka,

as Baldas 'puckers up'....

...and moves in for the kill.

Another successful Hogmanay!
