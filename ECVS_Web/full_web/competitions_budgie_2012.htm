The Budgie Cup 2012 was held in the ECVS clubrooms on Friday 17th February 2012.

The 2012 competition was judged by Toby Sigouin.

This year's judge, Toby Sigouin, commented on the high standard of entries this year, and awarded several Highly Commended Awards as well as selecting an overall winner.

The 2012 Budgie Cup Winner was Bryce Morrison with his photo titled "It's home from fishing we go".
