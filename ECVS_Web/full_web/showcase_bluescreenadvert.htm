Showcase Film - Introduction to ECVS (Bluescreen Experiment)

"Introduction to ECVS", is a short advertisement produced by ECVS members during a hands-on film-making evening on Thursday 2nd April 2009. This film was made using our bluescreen room.

Several members listed the benefits of ECVS membership and we used chromakey to replace the background with photos and film clips of ECVS facilities and productions.

We used the free Squirlz Morph package to morph the faces of the subjects into one another.
