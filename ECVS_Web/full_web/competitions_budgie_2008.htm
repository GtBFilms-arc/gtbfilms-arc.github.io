The Budgie Cup is ECVS's only annual competition which does not involve film-making. This competition is for the best still photograph. Originally there was a requirement that all photographs must be in the form of slide transparancies, however this has been relaxed in recent years and we now accept entries in any format - slide, print or digital. These are then converted to photo-CD for projection.

Each year, members are allowed to enter six photographs which should be author identified correctly, the entries are assessed by an independant judge, often a member of Edinburgh Photographic Society. The judge will then make comments on each of the projected photos on the night, before revealing the winner.
