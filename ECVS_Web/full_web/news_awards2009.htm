The Annual ECVS Awards Night was held on Friday 24th April 2009.

Bob Bell introduced the evening, revealing the winners of the various club trophies in this years Annual competition. Sean Groat had spent a lot of time and effort decorating the club for this special event, and laying on a wide selection of food and drink for attendees.

Following the announcement of prize winners, a selection of winning films were shown.

The evening was well attended by members and their friends and family.
