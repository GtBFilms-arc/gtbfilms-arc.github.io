The Bridges Trophy is a friendly competition between Edinburgh Cine and Video Society and Dunfermline Camcorder Club, run (usually) yearly since 1988. Each society creates a showreel of members films, the total running time of which should be no more than 45 minutes. An independent judge then watches both showreels and awards the prize to the society with the best selection of films. N.B. this award is based on the variety and quality of the entire showreel, not on the individual films.

The host for this event switches each year between the two societies, with members from each society travelling to the other on the night of the event. For the next competition (2012) the host will be Dunfermline.
