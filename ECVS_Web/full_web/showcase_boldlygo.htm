Showcase Film - To Boldly Go

"To Boldly Go", is a film produced by Sean Groat for an ECVS 5-minute video night. The film is the first in a number of short films produced by Sean on a sci-fi theme, charting the adventures of Space-cadet Alan as he visits strange new worlds.
