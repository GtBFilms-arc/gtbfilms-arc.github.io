The ECVS Annual General Meeting for the 2009/2010 session was held on Thursday 29th April 2010.

The AGM was reasonably well attended, and the new committee for 2010/2011 session was duly elected. The club finances were reviewed, and the outgoing President, Sean Groat, gave his review of the year. Several matters appertaining to the society were discussed and voted on by the members.
