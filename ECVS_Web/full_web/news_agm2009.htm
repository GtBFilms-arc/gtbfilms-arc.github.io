The ECVS Annual General Meeting for the 2008/2009 session was held on Thursday 23rd April 2009.

The AGM was reasonably well attended, and the new committee for 2009/2010 session was duly elected. The club finances were reviewed, and the outgoing President, Bob Bell, gave his review of the year. Several matters appertaining to the society were discussed and voted on by the members.
