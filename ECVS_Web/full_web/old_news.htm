22nd January 2009
Video from Stills
Jim Closs presented a very interesting evening on the techniques he used to produce video from still images. While many film-makers use stills in their films, one must be careful to prevent the finished film turning into a slide-show. Jim showed the club the methods he used to capture high quality images, and then how to introduce movement into these images to create dynamic and interesting footage.

An accompanying tutorial will shortly be included in the March 2009 issue of Cine-Chat, and also made available via this website.

5th May 2008
Breaking the Silence
This is a documentary film made by ECVS members, Bob Bell, Drew Fleming, Norman Speirs and John Clark, and relates to the history of the Georgian Bells originally installed in the church of St Andrew's and St George's at 13 George Street, Edinburgh.  The eight bells , made at the Whitechapel Bell Foundry in London rang weekly, from 1788 to 1903, until silenced due to safety reasons. After being refurbished, re-tuned and the steeple area strengthened the bells were re- installed in 2006.
