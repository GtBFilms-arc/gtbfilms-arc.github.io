5th December 2013
The Art of Cinematography
Alan Brown presented an evening entitled 'The Art of Cinematography'. Based on the book 'Cinematic Storytelling', by Jennifer Van Sijil, the evening consisted of the discussion of various cinematic techniques, used to convey meaning or atmosphere without the use of dialogue, illustrated by a number of short clips of the techniques in use in commercial films.

7th November 2013
Edinburgh's Silver Screens
Stewart Emm took ECVS members back in time, on a virtual tour of Edinburgh, when cinema was in it's heyday. Many of the buildings we recognise today were infact cinemas in the past. In addition, many of the locations used as film locations over the years were pointed out an illustrated with clips from the movies.
